The Six Mobile Gotchas

Common misconceptions in mobile app development

“The Six Gotchas: Common Misconceptions in Mobile Application Development” debunks the assumptions and flat-out falsities of the mobile app development world. You'll learn how to identify and avoid these mistakes and create a mobile strategy based in reality.

This white paper uncovers and addresses myths including:

  • The best apps are built one at a time
  • Wrappers are apps, right?
  • Blackberry is dead
  • Mobile web is enough
  • 3rd party development costs are manageable and predictable

Agree that these are gotchas? Not sure they're not correct? Read "The Six Gotchas" to find out why this "common knowledge" isn't so knowledgeable, and what you can do to get mobile right.
