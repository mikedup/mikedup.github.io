Kony Management Cloud Product Highlights

Watch a brief overview of the Kony Management Cloud, the least intrusive and most context-aware EMM solution on the market.

Hide transcript of this video

The first generation of mobile management arrived just in time for the mobile tsunami, but today point solutions are no longer what enterprises need. IT's problem isn’t the new devices and applications; it’s what everyone is doing with them. Mobility has evolved and brute force is no longer acceptable.

To ensure the security of the business, IT needs a smarter, context aware, integrated approach. Policies must be managed at the individual level in order to protect the company from its core to the edge of the enterprise. Why is this important? Because mobility affects everyone.

CIOs must support an increasingly mobile environment while mitigating risks posed by a wide variety of devices. IT operations want to minimize complexity by managing apps, devices and content in a single integrated environment. And security managers, well, their job was hard enough before all these devices came in. Now they need ways to stay ahead of today’s variables and be ready for the next shiny new thing that the employees will bring to work tomorrow.

That’s why we created the Kony Management Cloud. As part of an integrated suite designed to support the entire lifecycle of enterprise mobility, the Kony Management Cloud helps IT manage mobile apps, devices and content across both BYOD or enterprise-owned device programs. It’s the least intrusive and most situation-aware enterprise mobility management suite on the market.

The Kony Management Cloud consists of mobile device management and mobile application management. Let’s take a look at some key features.

Mobile device management offers full control over device hardware and data flow in and out of the device. Administrators can quickly view compliant status of all enrolled devices and take remedial actions to locate, lock, reset, or wipe the device. Wi-Fi, VpN and other settings can be managed without user intervention and device features like a camera can be disabled based on geolocation rules to secure sensitive documents or work areas. And don’t worry about BYOD; with the Kony Management Cloud, enterprise and personal data can stay completely separate.

Mobile application management provides surgical control over enterprise applications. The Kony Management Cloud lets you secure and deploy private apps using an enterprise app store. Apply time and date, geolocation and other policies to individual apps and push app updates to keep all your users on the same release. And, like mobile device management, you can do it all separately and securely, wiping only the app data without affecting personal user data.

Each component of the Kony Management Cloud is pulled together through a centralized intelligent dashboard. There, you can review compliant status of all enrolled devices, easily manage policies and set up automated policy compliance actions, leverage app accessible management tools, and manage push notifications and subscription messages.

Isn’t it time for smarter enterprise mobility management? So, go to Kony.com and try the Kony Management Cloud. You’ll manage apps, devices, and content securely, accurately, and with the most situation-aware technology on the market. And instead of worrying about your user’s shiny new thing, you’ll have more time to enjoy yours.

Glossary detail module

App development

“App” is a term that has become popularized in the last few years, especially among mobile phone users. App is short for application, and the business of app development has grown to impressive heights over a relatively short period of time. Apps, as we know them from a few years ago, were originally developed in order to be used for small, handheld devices such as personal digital assistants (PDAs). That has changed somewhat, as smartphones have mostly replaced PDAs. Additionally, tablets have become a popular alternative to traditional computing, leading to an even more accelerated development of apps.

Apps can be used for an incalculable variety of reasons, from mobile banking to checking show times for a particular movie at the local cinema. There is a high demand for new apps, and customers want them to manage many key aspects of their everyday life. This demand for variety has led almost all companies to get into the app development game. From Joe and Ann’s mom and pop shop, to Google and Apple and Berkshire Hathaway, companies are in a never-ending struggle to develop the latest and greatest app. However, app development does not stop with these companies, almost all other businesses and private developers are also contributing to the ever-growing app market.
